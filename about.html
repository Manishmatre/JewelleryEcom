Our Story

At ShilpoKotha, we believe that jewelry is more than an accessory—it's an expression of identity, a celebration of craftsmanship, and a connection to tradition. Our journey began with a passion for preserving the artistry of handcrafted jewelry while bringing it into the modern world.

Since 2015

The Beginning of ShilpoKotha

Founded in 2015 by jewelry designer Anika Rahman, ShilpoKotha (meaning "House of Art" in Bengali) began as a small workshop in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Anika's vision was to combine traditional South Asian jewelry-making techniques with contemporary designs that appeal to the modern woman.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand known for its unique designs, ethical sourcing practices, and commitment to preserving traditional craftsmanship while embracing innovation.

Our Values

At the heart of ShilpoKotha are the values that guide every piece we create and every decision we make.

Artisanal Craftsmanship

We honor traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring each piece is crafted with care and precision by skilled artisans.

Ethical Sourcing

We are committed to responsible sourcing of materials, ensuring our gemstones and metals are ethically obtained and our practices environmentally sustainable.

Community Impact

We believe in giving back to the communities that inspire us. A portion of our profits supports education and training for aspiring artisans.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Each ShilpoKotha piece is a testament to the meticulous craftsmanship that goes into creating fine jewelry. Our artisans combine traditional techniques with modern precision to create pieces that are both timeless and contemporary.

Ethically Sourced Materials

We use only the finest ethically sourced gemstones and recycled precious metals in our creations.

Traditional Techniques

Our artisans employ age-old techniques like filigree, meenakari (enameling), and hand-setting to create intricate details.

Quality Assurance

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our exacting standards before reaching you.

Our Journey

From a small workshop to an internationally recognized brand, our journey has been one of passion, perseverance, and artistry.

2015

The Beginning

ShilpoKotha was founded in Dhaka, Bangladesh by Anika Rahman with a small team of 3 artisans.

2017

First Flagship Store

Opened our first physical store in Dhaka, showcasing our complete collection of handcrafted jewelry.

2019

International Recognition

Featured in Vogue and received the South Asian Artisan Excellence Award for our innovative designs.

2021

Global Expansion

Launched our international e-commerce platform and expanded our team to 15 artisans.

2023

Sustainability Pledge

Committed to using 100% recycled metals and ethically sourced gemstones in all our creations.

2025

Today & Beyond

Continuing our mission to create beautiful, meaningful jewelry while supporting artisan communities and sustainable practices.
