Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact us.

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and typically takes 1-2 business days. International shipping is available to select countries.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition with all packaging and tags. Custom orders are non-refundable unless there is a defect in craftsmanship.

Yes, we offer custom jewelry design services. Our team of skilled artisans can create a unique piece based on your specifications. Please contact our customer service team or visit one of our stores to discuss your custom order requirements.

To keep your jewelry looking its best, we recommend storing pieces separately to prevent scratching, removing jewelry before swimming or bathing, and cleaning regularly with a soft cloth. For specific care instructions for your piece, please refer to the care card included with your purchase.

Yes, we offer repair services for our jewelry. Please bring your piece to one of our stores or contact our customer service team to arrange for repair. Repair costs will vary depending on the type of repair needed.
